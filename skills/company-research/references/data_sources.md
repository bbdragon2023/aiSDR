# Data Sources for Company Research

## Public Databases
- **Crunchbase** - Funding, investors, company info
- **LinkedIn** - Company pages, employee counts, decision makers
- **Glassdoor** - Employee reviews, salary data, culture insights
- **G2/Capterra** - Product reviews and comparisons
- **PitchBook** - Private company financials (paid)

## Technology Research
- **BuiltWith** - Website technology detection
- **StackShare** - Tech stacks shared by companies
- **GitHub** - Open source activity
- **Job postings** - Technologies mentioned in requirements

## News and Press
- **Google News** - Recent coverage
- **Company newsroom** - Official press releases
- **TechCrunch, VentureBeat** - Startup and tech news
- **Industry publications** - Vertical-specific news

## Financial Information
- **SEC filings** - Public company financials
- **Yahoo Finance** - Stock data and analyst estimates
- **PitchBook/CB Insights** - Private company estimates

## Social Signals
- **Twitter/X** - Company and executive accounts
- **LinkedIn posts** - Thought leadership content
- **YouTube** - Product demos, interviews
- **Podcasts** - Executive appearances

## Competitive Intelligence
- **SimilarWeb** - Website traffic estimates
- **SEMrush/Ahrefs** - SEO and marketing analysis
- **Owler** - Competitive comparisons
