# Email Templates Reference

## By Persona

### C-Suite (CEO, CFO, CRO)
- Ultra-brief (under 75 words)
- Lead with business outcome
- Mention peer companies
- Direct CTA for delegate or direct meeting

### VP/Director Level
- Brief but can include more context
- Focus on team-level outcomes
- Reference their specific initiatives
- Offer flexibility in engagement

### Manager/Individual Contributor
- Can be slightly longer
- More tactical focus
- Reference day-to-day challenges
- Lower-commitment CTA

## By Trigger Event

### New in Role
```
Subject: Congrats on the new role at [Company]

Hi [Name],

Congrats on stepping into the [Title] role at [Company] - exciting times!

When leaders join new teams, they often [common challenge]. I've helped several [similar titles] at companies like [example] tackle this by [brief approach].

Would it be helpful to share what's worked for others in your shoes?

[Signature]
```

### Funding Announcement
```
Subject: Idea for scaling [area] post-Series [X]

Hi [Name],

Congrats on the Series [X] - [$ amount] to [stated goal] is exciting.

With that growth, [common scaling challenge] usually becomes urgent. We've helped [similar company] [specific outcome] during their post-funding scale.

Worth comparing notes?

[Signature]
```

### Job Posting Signal
```
Subject: Re: your [Role] search

Hi [Name],

Noticed you're hiring [Roles] - usually means [inferred priority/challenge].

Rather than adding headcount, some teams use [your approach] to [outcome]. [Company] did this and [result].

Open to a quick chat about what might work for [their company]?

[Signature]
```

### Company News/Launch
```
Subject: Re: [Company]'s launch of [Product/Initiative]

Hi [Name],

Saw the news about [launch/initiative] - [specific observation or compliment].

As you scale [related area], [challenge] often comes up. We've helped teams like [example] handle this by [approach].

Would it be useful to swap notes?

[Signature]
```

## Follow-Up Sequences

### Sequence 1: Standard (4 touches over 2 weeks)
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 4: Value-add follow-up
- Day 8: New angle/question
- Day 14: Breakup

### Sequence 2: Executive (3 touches over 3 weeks)
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 7: Brief bump with new insight
- Day 21: Respectful breakup

### Sequence 3: Warm Lead (5 touches over 3 weeks)
- Day 1: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Quick follow-up
- Day 7: Share relevant content
- Day 14: New angle
- Day 21: Breakup with value

## Subject Line Formulas

### Curiosity
- "Quick question about [their initiative]"
- "Idea for [Company]"
- "[Mutual connection] mentioned you"

### Relevance
- "Re: your [topic] post"
- "Saw [Company]'s news about [event]"
- "For [Company]'s [team/initiative]"

### Value
- "[Outcome] for [Company]'s [team]"
- "How [Similar Company] solved [challenge]"
- "[Resource] for [their role/challenge]"

### Direct
- "15 min this week?"
- "[Topic] - worth discussing?"
- "Connecting re: [topic]"
