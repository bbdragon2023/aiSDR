# Prospect Enrichment Guide

## Primary Data Sources

### LinkedIn
- Current and past positions
- Education and certifications
- Skills and endorsements
- Recommendations
- Activity (posts, articles, comments)
- Connections and followers

### Company Website
- Team/about pages with bios
- Press releases mentioning them
- Blog posts they've authored
- Speaking or event pages

### Professional Platforms
- Twitter/X - Professional commentary
- GitHub - Technical contributions
- Medium/Substack - Thought leadership
- Speaker profiles on conference sites

### News and Media
- Interviews and quotes
- Bylined articles
- Podcast appearances
- Awards and recognition

## Enrichment Checklist

Before outreach, ensure you have:

- [ ] Verified current role and company
- [ ] Understood their responsibilities
- [ ] Found recent activity or news
- [ ] Identified 2-3 personalization angles
- [ ] Confirmed they're a decision maker/influencer
- [ ] Checked for mutual connections
- [ ] Noted any timing considerations

## Quality Signals

**High-quality prospect data includes:**
- Verified within last 30 days
- Multiple source confirmation
- Clear decision-making authority
- Active professional presence
- Relevant industry experience

**Low-quality prospect data has:**
- Stale information (6+ months old)
- Single source only
- Unclear role or authority
- No recent activity
- Mismatched industry background
